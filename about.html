Norway rules. Black Metal makes a lot more sense now.

I'm Trey McKay, and I'm an Interaction Designer at Shapeways in NYC. Originally from Houston, TX, I came to NYC after studying Graphic Design for four years at SCAD in Savannah, GA. My mind is most at peace when I'm learning something new, diving deep into a fictional world, or making something cool like art or music.

2016 has taught me a lot so far, so it's been a good year. I took up boxing almost two years ago, and had my first fight for charity in May (I lost but it's ok). I traveled alone for a few weeks this summer, which was also a first. Three weeks exploring the Nordics on my own? Sign me up. Learned a ton about myself, meeting new people, and having a good time.

I was interviewed by Liz at Series Digital - Part 1, Part 2, which was a first. I've spoken about design on two occasions in the last year, the first was on a panel at General Assembly (link), and the second was at Dev Bootcamp NYC, where I spoke about the "Basics of UX Design for Developers."
