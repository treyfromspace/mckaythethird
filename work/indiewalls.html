2015 - PRESENT INDIEWALLS

Starting in July of 2015, I've helped the NYC-based startup Indiewalls improve their software product that helps interior designers source original artwork.

I've helped them by designing and establishing interface patterns, improving usability of core flows, and establishing brand voice through visual design.

In short

As the only designer working on Indiewalls' product, I've been responsible for designing solutions to customer pain points, establishing usability best practices, and improving overall quality of visual design.

My focus

Working together with product managers, software developers, and Indiewalls' CEO, I've designed improvements for the homepage and other key landing pages, project creation and management flows, cart and checkout-like experiences, the blog, art discovery, and artwork inventory management for artists.
